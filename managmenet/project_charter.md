# Project Charter

## Project Title
E-commerce Customer Behavior & Operational Analytics Project

## Background
An e-commerce business is experiencing inconsistent revenue, delayed deliveries, and declining customer retention. Leadership requires data-driven insights to improve operations, customer satisfaction, and product strategy.

## Problem Statement
The business lacks centralized analytics to understand:
- Customer repeat behavior
- Delivery delays
- Product performance
- Seller performance
- Revenue distribution

Without structured analytics, decision-making is reactive instead of strategic.

## Objectives
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns
- Identify operational bottlenecks
- Evaluate seller and product performance
- Build executive dashboards
- Provide product and operational recommendations

## Stakeholders
- Business Leadership
- Operations Team
- Product Team
- Marketing Team
- Sellers/Vendors

## Scope
Included:
- Data cleaning & transformation
- SQL-based analysis
- Power BI dashboards
- Business documentation
- Product recommendations

Excluded:
- Live production deployment
- Machine learning automation

## Deliverables
- Cleaned dataset
- SQL analysis scripts
- Business insights document
- Power BI dashboard
- Business Requirement Document (BRD)
- Final case study presentation

## Risks
- Data inconsistencies
- Missing values
- Limited real-time data
- Time constraints

## Timeline
Total Duration: 7 Days
- Day 1: Planning & Setup
- Day 2–3: Data Cleaning & Analysis
- Day 4: Dashboard Development
- Day 5–6: Documentation & Insights
- Day 7: Final Case Study & Portfolio
